About

A little about me

I’m a backend-focused software engineer who likes making complex systems feel simple, steady, and useful.

At Victaulic, I help modernize internal platforms into cleaner, more scalable services using NestJS, Angular, SQL, and Azure. A lot of that work means untangling legacy systems, improving background jobs, and making integrations easier for teams to depend on.

I’m also building AISeq, where I lead the architecture of a wellness genomics platform with TypeScript, PostgreSQL, Prisma, and clean architecture. That work includes shaping versioned data models and building APIs around structured, workflow-driven experiences.

The common thread in my work is backend systems: APIs, workflows, integrations, and data-heavy platforms that need to stay understandable as they grow.

Outside of engineering, I like traveling, photography, and cars, especially older BMWs and Porsches. I also enjoy working on my own E46, which probably says a lot about how much I like understanding how things are put together.
