What keeps me engaged isn’t just writing clean code — it’s finding leverage. Whether that’s automating a deployment, smoothing out a team process, or simplifying a UI, I’m always looking for ways to make systems (and people) work better together.

Outside of work, I’m into fitness, gaming (especially RPGs and optimization), and long-term financial planning. I recently moved to Jersey City with my wife and am currently working toward homeownership and growing into a leadership role in tech.
